Yada Towrah

Updated: 2026-01-03

Inspired by imagery on the lead tablet found on Mt Ebal dating back to the time of the Exodus, and historic samples of pictograph Hebrew, Yada Yah has created a font that it feels represents the underlying symbology of each letter. It also incorporates spacing and scale amenable to inclusion within the Yada Yah bookshelves, and is replacing the Semitic Early font.

Jupiter Yada

Updated: 2025-12-27

A decorative font used throughout the Yada Yah volumes, that is a combination of multiple versions of the Jupiter family of fonts. This facilitates the selection of various letter variations, and improves the spacing and proportions involved in making attractive headings and titles throughout the books.
