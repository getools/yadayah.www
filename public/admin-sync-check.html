Automated sync verification. The tool plays the YouTube video at sampled timestamps, captures the tab audio, and compares the audio onset against the MP3 at the same timestamp. Binary search narrows the desync point until the gap between the last known good and last known bad point is under 30 seconds. If drift is found, the MP3 is reset to a Partial recording ending at the last good point — you can then re-record from there to fix the audio.
