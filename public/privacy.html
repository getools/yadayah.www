Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

Introduction

YadaYah.com ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website or use our Community features.

Information We Collect

When you sign in to our Community using a third-party authentication provider (Google, Facebook, or Apple), we receive and store the following information from your account:

  • Name — your display name as provided by the authentication service
  • Email address — used to identify your account
  • Profile picture — displayed alongside your Community posts
  • Provider account ID — a unique identifier used to link your login to your account on our site

We do not access your contacts, friends lists, posts, photos, or any other data from your social media accounts.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected solely for the following purposes:

  • To create and maintain your Community account
  • To display your name and profile picture alongside your discussion posts and replies
  • To prevent duplicate accounts by matching email addresses

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not use cookies for advertising or tracking purposes
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties
  • We do not use your data for marketing or promotional emails
  • We do not collect payment or financial information

Data Storage and Security

Your account information is stored securely on our servers. We use encrypted connections (HTTPS) for all data transmission. Access to user data is restricted to site administrators.

Third-Party Authentication

When you sign in using Google, Facebook, or Apple, your authentication is handled entirely by that provider. We never receive or store your password. Each provider's own privacy policy governs how they handle your data during the login process:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Facebook Privacy Policy
  • Apple Privacy Policy

Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Upon request, we will remove your account, display name, email, profile picture, and all Community posts and replies associated with your account.

Children's Privacy

Our website and Community features are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to request data deletion, please use the Ask page to contact us.
